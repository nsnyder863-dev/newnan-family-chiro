Common Causes

Pregnancy-related discomfort may be connected to posture changes, weight distribution, joint stress, muscle tension, hip tightness, or changes in daily movement.

Symptoms Patients May Notice

Lower back pain, hip discomfort, pelvic pressure, sciatic-type symptoms, stiffness, or trouble getting comfortable while sitting or sleeping.

How Chiropractic Care May Help

Chiropractic care during pregnancy should be comfortable, conservative, and adapted to the patient's stage of pregnancy. Care may include gentle techniques, positioning support, and movement guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?

No. In Georgia, patients do not need a referral to schedule chiropractic care.

Do you accept insurance?

Newnan Family Chiropractic accepts many major insurance plans. Call the office to verify benefits before your visit.

When should I seek urgent care?

Seek urgent medical care for severe pain, major trauma, weakness, numbness, fever, unexplained weight loss, or bowel or bladder changes.
