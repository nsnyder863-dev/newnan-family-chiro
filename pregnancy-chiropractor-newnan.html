Supporting Comfort

Pregnancy changes how your body moves.

As pregnancy progresses, posture, balance, and weight distribution naturally change. These changes may contribute to discomfort in the back, hips, pelvis, or neck. Chiropractic care may help improve mobility and manage musculoskeletal symptoms.

Our Approach

Gentle, individualized care.

Every pregnancy is different. Dr. Truong evaluates your symptoms, health history, and stage of pregnancy before recommending care. Treatment is modified to provide appropriate, comfortable positioning throughout your visit.

Our goal is to help improve comfort and mobility while supporting your overall well-being during pregnancy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Pregnancy FAQ

Is chiropractic care during pregnancy safe?

Many pregnant patients receive chiropractic care. Treatment recommendations should always be individualized based on your health, symptoms, and pregnancy. If you have questions or a high-risk pregnancy, discuss them with both your obstetric provider and Dr. Truong.

Can chiropractic care help with pregnancy-related back pain?

Many patients seek chiropractic care to help manage pregnancy-related musculoskeletal discomfort and improve mobility.

Do I need a referral?

Most patients do not need a referral for chiropractic care, though insurance requirements may vary.
