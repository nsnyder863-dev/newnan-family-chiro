Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making our website accessible to as many visitors as possible.

Our Commitment

Newnan Family Chiropractic is committed to providing a website that is accessible and usable for all visitors, including individuals with disabilities. We continually work to improve accessibility and the overall user experience.

Accessibility Features

  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
  • Readable typography and high color contrast.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation where practical.
  • Alternative text for important images.
  • Clear headings and page structure.

Need Assistance?

If you experience difficulty using this website or need assistance accessing any information, please contact our office. We will do our best to provide the information or assistance you need.
