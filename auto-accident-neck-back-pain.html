Neck and Back Pain After an Auto Accident

Educational information from Newnan Family Chiropractic.

Pain after an auto accident may appear immediately or develop over hours or days. Neck stiffness, back pain, headaches, and soreness are common reasons patients seek evaluation. Emergency symptoms such as chest pain, loss of consciousness, severe weakness, numbness, or trouble breathing require urgent medical attention.

When to call the office

If symptoms are affecting your daily life, call Newnan Family Chiropractic at (770) 683-9105 or send a general message through the contact page.
