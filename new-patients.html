What to Expect on Your First Visit

We make your first appointment simple, comfortable, and focused entirely on your needs.

Your First Visit

During your first appointment, we take time to understand your health history, current symptoms, and goals. This allows us to create a care plan tailored specifically to you.

Consultation & Exam

We perform a detailed chiropractic examination to identify the root cause of your discomfort. This may include posture analysis, range of motion testing, and spinal evaluation.

Personalized Care Plan

After your exam, we explain our findings in clear, simple terms and outline a recommended treatment plan.

Insurance & Payment

We work with many insurance plans and offer flexible payment options to make care accessible.
