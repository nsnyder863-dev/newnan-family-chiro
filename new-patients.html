What You Can Expect

Health History

We'll discuss your health history, symptoms, lifestyle, and goals to better understand your concerns.

Please Bring

Before Your Appointment

Photo ID

Bring a valid government-issued photo identification.

Insurance Card

If using insurance, please bring your current insurance card.

Medication List

Bring a list of current medications if applicable.

Questions

Write down any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss during your visit.

Insurance

Insurance & VA Community Care

We accept most major insurance plans. If you're planning to use insurance, we recommend contacting our office before your appointment so we can help verify your benefits.

VA Community Care Patients
If you're using VA Community Care benefits, authorization from the VA may be required before covered treatment begins. Our staff will be happy to answer questions about the process.
