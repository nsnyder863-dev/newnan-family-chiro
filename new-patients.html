2

3

Evaluation & Recommendations

Insurance & VA

Most major insurance accepted.

Call the office to verify benefits before your visit. VA Community Care patients are welcome, but VA authorization may be required before covered care begins.

Medical Emergency Notice

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, severe trauma, chest pain, weakness, numbness, or loss of bowel/bladder control, call 911 or seek emergency medical care.
