What Care Feels Like

Clear explanations before treatment begins.

Your visit starts with a conversation and examination. Dr. Truong explains what he found, whether chiropractic care appears appropriate, and what the proposed approach involves.

Techniques and visit frequency depend on the individual. You are encouraged to ask questions, discuss comfort preferences, and understand the plan before proceeding.

Emergency Guidance

Some symptoms need immediate medical care.

Call 911 or seek urgent medical evaluation for severe trauma, chest pain, stroke-like symptoms, sudden weakness or numbness, loss of bowel or bladder control, fainting, or a sudden severe headache. Do not use an appointment request form for an emergency.
