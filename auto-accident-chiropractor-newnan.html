After an Accident

Symptoms don't always appear immediately.

Following a motor vehicle accident, some people feel fine at first but develop pain or stiffness hours or even days later. An evaluation can help determine whether chiropractic care may be appropriate.

Whiplash

Neck pain and stiffness commonly associated with rear-end collisions.

Back Pain

Pain or muscle tightness affecting the upper or lower back.

Headaches

Headaches sometimes develop following neck strain or injury.

Reduced Mobility

Difficulty turning, bending, lifting, or performing everyday activities.

Our Approach

Frequently Asked Questions

Auto Accident FAQ

Should I be evaluated even if I don't feel much pain?

Some injuries become more noticeable after the initial shock wears off. If you've been involved in an accident, it's reasonable to seek an evaluation if symptoms develop.

Do you work with personal injury cases?

Yes. Newnan Family Chiropractic welcomes patients receiving care related to personal injury and motor vehicle accidents.

When should I seek emergency care?

Call 911 or seek immediate medical attention for severe trauma, loss of consciousness, chest pain, difficulty breathing, significant weakness, numbness, or any life-threatening symptoms.
