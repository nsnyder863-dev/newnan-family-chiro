Pregnancy, Back Pain, and Hip Discomfort

Educational information from Newnan Family Chiropractic.

Pregnancy changes posture, weight distribution, and joint stress. Some patients seek chiropractic care for back or hip discomfort during pregnancy. Care should be individualized, gentle, and coordinated with the patient’s healthcare team when needed, especially for high-risk pregnancies.

When to call the office

If symptoms are affecting your daily life, call Newnan Family Chiropractic at (770) 683-9105 or send a general message through the contact page.
