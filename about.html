About Dr. Truong

Experienced care with a personal approach.

For more than 25 years, Dr. Tuan Truong has cared for patients throughout Newnan and Coweta County. His approach begins with listening, understanding the concern, and determining whether chiropractic care is appropriate.

Patients receive explanations in clear language and recommendations based on their symptoms, examination findings, daily activities, and goals. The practice welcomes questions and avoids a one-size-fits-all approach.
