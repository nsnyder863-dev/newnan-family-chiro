About Dr. Truong

Practical care, clear explanations, and individualized plans.

For more than 25 years, Dr. Truong has cared for patients throughout Newnan and Coweta County. His approach centers on listening, evaluating carefully, and recommending care based on each patient’s symptoms and goals.

Patients often seek care for back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, sports-related discomfort, personal injury cases, pregnancy-related discomfort, pediatric concerns, and wellness support.
