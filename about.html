About Dr. Truong

Experienced Chiropractic Care

For more than 25 years, Dr. Tuan Truong has proudly cared for patients throughout Newnan and Coweta County. His approach focuses on listening to each patient, understanding their concerns, and creating individualized treatment plans that support improved movement and day-to-day function.

Every patient has different goals. Whether someone is seeking care for back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, sports-related injuries, or general wellness, Dr. Truong believes treatment should be personalized rather than one-size-fits-all.

His commitment is to provide compassionate, patient-centered care in a welcoming environment where every visitor feels heard and respected.
