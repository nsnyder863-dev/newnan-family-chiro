Notice of Privacy Practices

Protecting your health information is an important part of the care we provide.

Your Privacy Matters

Newnan Family Chiropractic is committed to protecting your personal health information in accordance with applicable federal and state privacy laws, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

How Your Information May Be Used

  • Providing treatment and coordinating your care.
  • Billing insurance companies and processing payments.
  • Office operations such as scheduling and quality improvement.
  • When required or permitted by law.

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your health records.
  • Request corrections to your health information when appropriate.
  • Request confidential communications.
  • Request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures.
  • Receive a copy of our complete Notice of Privacy Practices.

Questions

If you have questions about how your information is handled, please contact our office at (770) 683-9105.

Important:

This webpage provides a general summary of our privacy practices. Patients may receive our complete Notice of Privacy Practices during their visit or upon request.
