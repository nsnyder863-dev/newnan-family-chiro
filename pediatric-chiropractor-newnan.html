Frequently Asked Questions

Pediatric Chiropractic FAQ

Is chiropractic care safe for children?

When appropriate, chiropractic care for children is modified to match the child's age, size, and individual needs. Parents are encouraged to discuss any questions or concerns during the visit.

Does my child need a referral?

Most children do not require a referral for chiropractic care, although some insurance plans may have specific requirements.

Will treatment be the same as an adult adjustment?

No. Pediatric chiropractic care is adapted for the child's age, development, and condition. Techniques used for children differ from those commonly used for adults.
