Patient Privacy & HIPAA Information

Newnan Family Chiropractor is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients. We recognize the importance of safeguarding personal health information and strive to handle it responsibly and in accordance with applicable federal and state privacy laws.

Your Privacy Matters

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect patient information. Access to patient records is limited to authorized personnel who require the information to provide healthcare services or perform related business functions.

Website Communication

This website is intended to provide general information about our practice and allow visitors to contact our office regarding appointments or general questions.

Please do not submit confidential medical information, insurance numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive health information through the website contact form.

Questions About Your Privacy

If you have questions regarding your privacy or would like additional information about our privacy practices, please contact our office directly during normal business hours.

Please note: This webpage provides general information about our commitment to patient privacy. Patients receive the practice's official Notice of Privacy Practices as required by applicable law.
