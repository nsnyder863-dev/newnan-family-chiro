Our Approach

Personalized care for active patients.

Dr. Truong evaluates how your symptoms affect movement and daily function. If chiropractic care is appropriate, treatment recommendations are based on your activity level, symptoms, and personal goals.

The goal is to help reduce discomfort, improve mobility, and support your ability to stay active safely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Sports Injury FAQ

Do I need to be an athlete to schedule sports injury care?

No. Many patients dealing with activity-related discomfort benefit from an evaluation, whether the issue came from sports, exercise, work, or daily movement.

Can chiropractic care help with mobility?

Chiropractic care may help improve spinal and joint mobility when restrictions or discomfort are present.

When should I seek medical care first?

Seek urgent medical care for fractures, severe swelling, inability to bear weight, major trauma, numbness, weakness, or worsening symptoms.
